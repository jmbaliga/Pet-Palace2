About Us

Paul's Pet Palace is an independent family owned and operated pet store located in Manchester, CT. It is our mission to provide our dogs, cats, and birds with a comfortable living environment until they are adopted or rescued and to provide families with happy, healthly pets.

Paul's Pet Palace prides itself on offering the best selection of healthy puppies, kittens, and birds in the greater Hartford area. Many breeds, shapes, sizes and colors of puppies, dogs, kittens, cats, and birds are available for adoption. Ask our knowledgeable staff to help you find the right pet to fit your lifestyle and personality.
